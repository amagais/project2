Food Assistance Program Participants

Click on the axes below to see how the graph changes

Hover on a datapoint for more info





The chart above is illustrating the percentage of a state population that participates in the following food assistance programs:

    WIC – Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, & Children
    National School Lunch – Covers 9-month average, excludes June through August
    School Breakfast – Covers 9-month average, excludes June through August
    Summer Food – Covers 3-month average, June through August only
    Child & Adult Care – Food program for children & adults receiving food assistance at childcare centers, family daycare homes, and adult-care centers
Please note, when selecting a year/program, the y-axis will change to fit the data charted (the axis values are not fixed).
